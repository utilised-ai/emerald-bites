Patrick Tobin

Food Writer & Culinary Explorer

About Me

Dia dhuit! I'm Patrick Tobin, born and bred in the heart of Dublin's fair city. With a fierce passion for our country's grub, I've spent the last decade and more roaming every corner of Ireland, from humble local spots serving mammies' recipes to our finest Michelin-starred establishments. Through my writing, I'm sharing the magic of Irish food - both the traditional dishes that'd put a smile on your face and the modern takes that are making waves across the globe.

Professional Background

Sure, I've had the good fortune of learning from the best. After cutting my teeth at the renowned Ballymaloe Cookery School and working in some of Ireland's finest kitchens, I found my true calling in sharing stories about our incredible food heritage. These days, you'll find my scribblings in papers and magazines both here at home and across the pond.

My Journey

My love affair with food started in my granddad's pub in County Clare, where I spent countless evenings by the hearth, listening to tales of old while savoring the most delicious Irish stew you could imagine. Those early days, watching locals gather for the craic and comfort food, taught me that Irish cuisine is about more than just the ingredients - it's about community, tradition, and the warm welcome that we're famous for.

My Mission

Through Emerald Bites, I'm on a mission to showcase the absolute best of Irish food, from the traditional dishes that'd warm your cockles to the creative new plates that are turning heads worldwide. I'm passionate about supporting our local heroes - the farmers, fishermen, artisan producers, and chefs who are putting Irish cuisine on the map while staying true to our roots.

Let's Connect

Sure, I'd love to hear from you! Whether you're curious about finding the perfect Irish soda bread recipe, want to share your favorite hidden gem of a restaurant, or fancy a chat about all things food, don't be a stranger. Drop me a line - the kettle's always on!
