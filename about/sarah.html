About Me

Hello! I'm Sarah O'Connor, a Dublin-based food writer with a passion for discovering and sharing Ireland's rich culinary heritage. For over a decade, I've been exploring the length and breadth of Ireland, from cozy rural pubs to Michelin-starred restaurants, documenting the evolution of Irish cuisine and the stories behind our food.

Professional Background

With a background in both professional cooking and food journalism, I bring a unique perspective to Irish food writing. After training at the Ballymaloe Cookery School and working in several notable Irish restaurants, I turned my attention to food writing, contributing to various national and international publications.

My Journey

My love for food began in my grandmother's kitchen in County Kerry, where I learned the importance of seasonal ingredients and traditional cooking methods. This early exposure to authentic Irish cooking has shaped my approach to food writing, always seeking to highlight the connection between our culinary heritage and modern innovations.

My Mission

Through Emerald Bites, I aim to showcase the best of Irish cuisine, from traditional dishes to modern interpretations. My goal is to help readers discover the incredible depth and diversity of Ireland's food scene, while supporting local producers and restaurants who are passionate about quality and sustainability.
